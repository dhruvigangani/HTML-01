About the Company

Our mission is to provide you with the best pre-1994 antique Polaroid cameras available. Our speciality is providing a carefully chosen range of antique Polaroid cameras that perfectly capture the spirit of the heyday of instant photography. Our goal is to preserve the ageless craft of analogue photography by bringing fans together with legendary models manufactured prior to 1994.

We have a wide selection of Polaroid cameras in our store, each with an own history and appeal. We make sure that every camera we provide is a monument to the quality and inventiveness of Polaroid's tradition, from the legendary SuperColor 1000 with its rainbow stripe design to the cutting-edge Polaroid OneStep Autofocus SE and the sturdy Polaroid R 500.

Beyond simply selling goods, we are dedicated to building a community of photographers who value the allure and nostalgia of instant film. This is the reason for our passion for antique cameras.
